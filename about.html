About Me

This is what I do.

Hi

My name is Matthias Altmann. I'm a developer and IT sec enthusiast.
I work at a software company called Micromata directly in the center of Germany, in a city named Kassel, in an amazing atmosphere.

I am organizer of the IT-Security Meetup Kassel, a community driven regular meeting about security topics.
We come together in the middle of every month, Wednesdays.
You're welcome to join us - it's free and open for everyone.
But be prepared - it could be fun and you might get infected by the security virus. :-)
